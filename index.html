About me

In 2018, I graduated from the University of Michigan with a degree in Political Science and a desire to because a lawyer but while I was studying for the LSAT I realized that Law School wasn't the right move for me. During this time I started to code again and this reignited my love of technology.


I enjoy creating things that will have an impact on peoples lives whether that is through learning or enabling people to do things that weren't possible for them before. I love learning new things and getting others to enjoy learning as much as I do is something I go out of my way to do.


I recently graduated from the fullstack web development program at Lambda School, and am looking for my first software engineering position
